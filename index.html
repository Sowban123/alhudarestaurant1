Special Dish

MANDHI BRIYANI

Mandi Biryani is a traditional Arabian dish, popular in Dubai and the Middle East, known for its distinct cooking method. It features tender meat, usually chicken or lamb, marinated in a blend of spices, and served over fragrant basmati rice. The dish is cooked in a special underground oven called a "tandoor," which gives it a unique smoky flavor. The meat is slow-cooked to ensure it is juicy and flavorful, while the rice absorbs the rich spices. Mandi Biryani is often garnished with nuts, raisins, and herbs, making it a flavorful and aromatic meal.
